Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorvite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously reich scambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a median heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to ther center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelet)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will contiune to cook on the plate until served)
